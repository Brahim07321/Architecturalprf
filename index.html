About

Architecture
is an act of
listening.

I am Karim Raouf, an architectural designer with over a decade of practice across residential, civic, and cultural commissions. My work begins not with sketches but with silence — listening to site, client, and material before a single line is drawn.

Trained at the École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture de Paris and tempered by practice in Casablanca, Dubai, and Berlin, I believe the best buildings answer a question the client didn't know they were asking.

"Good architecture refuses to be invisible — it changes the people who pass through it."
